# CX Today: CRM and Customer Data Trends for 2026

Source: https://www.cxtoday.com/crm/crm-customer-data-trends-2026/
Fetched: March 2026

## Core thesis
"CRM is shifting from system of record to system of action. Customer data is the fuel. AI is the accelerator."

## Key findings

- Gartner Magic Quadrant for Sales Force Automation (2025): Leaders are Salesforce, Microsoft, Oracle
  - Directive: "shortlist vendors that make selling easier, not more structured"
  - Focus: reducing manual updates, improving forecasting, accelerating pipeline movement

- Forrester CRM Wave Q1 2025: Market shifting toward simplification and UX
  - "Bloated CRMs are losing patience"
  - Critical metric: adoption drives ROI

- SelectHub CRM Trends 2026: AI integration and analytics dominating
  - Teams buy features but reporting stays unreliable (data quality problem)

- Forbes Tech Council: AI agents now embedded natively in Salesforce, SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics
  - Agents perform actions, not just recommendations

## Recommended implementation path
1. Pick one high-volume workflow (lead handoff, renewals, case routing)
2. Map where customer context is lost
3. Establish identity and data quality for that workflow
4. Deploy AI where friction reduction occurs quickly
5. Track outcomes, not activities

## Critical warning
"One trap to avoid: buying 'more CRM' to solve 'bad data.'"
