# LockboxHQ V1 Scope (First 10 Paying Agents)

## Must-Have
- Reliable questionnaire intake to CRM pipeline
- Reliable manual lead add/update without duplicates
- Lead list + lead detail + stage updates
- Basic performance metrics with available deal fields
- Receptionist V1 SMS/call event ingestion into same lead model
- Communication history visible inside lead workspace
- Safe settings for receptionist behavior

## V1 Success Criteria
- Agents can publish one intake link and see new submissions appear in CRM
- Repeat submissions update existing lead records by identity
- Missed call/inbound SMS events create or update leads and log interactions
- Call/Text buttons in CRM are operational or clearly explain blockers
- No silent failures in intake routes

## Explicitly Out of Scope
- Live AI voice answering
- Advanced call center workflows
- Billing/subscription logic
- Team/brokerage commission split engine
- Full transaction management/back-office workflows
